the control for custom validation should be regular expression validator:-

for capital and small alphabits
(^[A-Za-z\s]+$)

for valid email id 
\w+([-+.']\w+)*@\w+([-.]\w+)*\.\w+([-.]\w+)*

For valid phone number of length 10
([0-9]{10}|[0-9]{10})
for numaric numbers
^[-+]?[0-9]*\.?[0-9]*([?[0-9]+)?$
only numbers 
([0-9]+)
